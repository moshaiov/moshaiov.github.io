Identity. [Jacobi] $\sum_{n\ge 1}e^{i(n^2\tau + nz)}=\prod_{k\ge 1}(1-q^{2k})(1+q^{2k-1}e^{iz})(1+q^{2k-1}e^{-iz})$ where $q=e^{i\tau}$ for all $z\in\C$, $\tau\in\U$.
