(sol 2) let $G$ be the graph on the prisoners with two connected if they share the same number. the $i^{\text{th}}$ prisoner guesses that the number of edges in $G$ is congruent to $i$ modulo $n$, leading to a unique guess for the number of connected prisoners. ▨
