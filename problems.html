problems

[⧉ 1] any given infinite sequence of real numbers contains an infinite monotonic subsequence.
[⧉ 2] let $a$ be a weakly increasing sequence of positive integers. if $a_n=o(n)$ then the sequence $n/a_n$ contains each positive integer.
[⧉ 3] a bounded harmonic function on $\mathbf{Z}^d$ must be constant.
($f:\mathbf{Z}^d\to\mathbf{R}$ is harmonic if $\forall x$ $f(x)=\underset{y\sim x}{\text{average}}f(y)$, the average being over the $2d$ neighboring vertices of $x$.)
[⧉ 4] an additive map $\mathbf{Z}^\mathbf{N}\to \mathbf{Z}$ (integer sequences to integers) must have the form $(x_n)_n\mapsto s_1x_1+\dots+s_kx_k$ for some $k,s_j$.
($f$ is additive if $f(x+y)=f(x)+f(y)$ $\forall x,y$)
[⧉ 5] a continuous function $f$ satisfies $\displaystyle\lim_{n\to\infty}f(na)=0$ for all $a$. show that $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}f(x)=0$.
[⧉ 6] a continuous $f:[0,1]\to\mathbf{R}$ satisfies $\displaystyle\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{f(x+h)+f(x-h)-2f(x)}{h^2}=0$ $\forall x\in(0,1)$. show that $f(x)=ax+b$ is affine.
[⧉ 7] a positif valued polynomial must have the form $R^2+S^2$ for two polynomials $R,S$.
[⧉ 8] Pooh has $2n+1$ honey pots. he noticed that he can eat any one of them and split the remaining into two groups of equal total weight, each with $n$ pots. show that the pots all weigh the same.
[⧉ 9] $a_{k+r}\le a_k+a_r$ $\forall n,m$ implies $a_n/n$ converges.
[⧉ 10] $n$ prisoners enter a room. the warden writes (different) real numbers on their foreheads. they can see the other, but not their own number. after a minute, the warden asks them to (simultaneously) raise either their right or their left arm. he orders them by the increasing numbers on their heads. if two consecutive prisoners raised the same hand, they are all killed. how can they be saved? (once they enter the room and see each other, no communication of any sort is allowed).
