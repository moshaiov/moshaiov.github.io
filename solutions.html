[Ⅎ 2] let $M_{ij}$ be $x$ on the diagonal and $1$ outside. then $\det M = \prod\text{eigen values} = (x-1)^{n-1}(x+n-1)$ equals by definition $\displaystyle\sum_{\sigma\in S_n}\text{sgn}(\sigma)x^{\#\text{fixed points}(\sigma)}$. now integrate on $[0,1]$ to get $\displaystyle \sum_{\sigma\in S_n}\dfrac{\text{sgn}(\sigma)}{1+\#\text{fixed points}(\sigma)}=(-1)^{n+1}\dfrac{n}{n+1}$.
